Privacy Policy

Chat (chat.4x.rip and the Chat mobile app, published by bough limited) is a bring-your-own-key client for AI chat APIs. It is designed so that your data stays on your device.

What stays on your device

  • API keys are stored only in your browser or app's local storage. They are never sent to our server, never synced, and never logged.
  • Conversations are stored locally on your device (IndexedDB).
  • Messages you send go directly from your device to the AI provider you configured, under that provider's privacy policy.

Optional account and sync

You can optionally sign in with GitHub to sync conversations across devices. If you do, we store your GitHub id, username, display name, and avatar URL, plus the conversations you choose to sync, on our server (Cloudflare D1/R2). We do not read GitHub data beyond your public profile, and we discard the GitHub access token immediately after sign-in. Deleting a conversation deletes it from the server. Signing out stops sync; you can disable it at any time in Settings.

Proxied requests

A few features route through our server only because the third-party service blocks direct browser requests (web search via Exa, and the OpenCode Zen provider). Those requests pass your key through per-request; it is not stored or logged.

Analytics

We collect anonymous, aggregate usage analytics (page views) that are not linked to your identity. No advertising, no tracking across other sites, no sale of data.
